DJ List
DJ Snake
DJ Snake is a Grammy-nominated producer and DJ known for his energetic tracks that fuse hip-hop, dance, and electronic music. He gained international fame with hits like "Turn Down for What" and "Taki Taki," showcasing his ability to blend genres and collaborate with top artists worldwide.
J Balvin
J Balvin is a Colombian reggaeton artist who has become a global ambassador for Latin music. Known for his vibrant style and infectious beats, he has achieved numerous chart-topping hits, including "Mi Gente" and "Ginza." Balvin's music incorporates elements of pop and hip-hop, making him one of the most influential figures in the genre today.
Calvin Harris
Calvin Harris is a Scottish DJ and producer celebrated for his innovative sound that merges electronic music with pop influences. With hits like "Summer" and "Feel So Close," he has collaborated with top artists across various genres, earning multiple awards and accolades, solidifying his position as one of the world's highest-paid DJs.
David Guetta
David Guetta is a French DJ and music producer known for his ability to blend electronic music with mainstream pop. With hits like "Titanium" featuring Sia and "When Love Takes Over," he has played a pivotal role in popularizing EDM globally and has collaborated with artists from multiple genres, making him a staple in the music industry.
Farruko
Farruko is a Puerto Rican artist known for his versatile style, combining reggaeton, Latin trap, and dancehall. His music often reflects his cultural roots, with hits like "Krippy Kush" and "Pepas," which showcase his dynamic approach to modern Latin music and his ability to connect with audiences across the globe.
Bad Bunny
Bad Bunny is a groundbreaking Puerto Rican artist whose music transcends genres, blending reggaeton, trap, and Latin pop. Known for his unique style and thought-provoking lyrics, he has rapidly become a global sensation with hits like "Dakiti" and "Yonaguni," pushing the boundaries of Latin music and gaining a dedicated fanbase worldwide.
Karol G
Karol G is a Colombian singer and songwriter recognized for her powerful voice and impactful lyrics. She has made significant contributions to the reggaeton and Latin pop scenes, with chart-topping hits like "Tusa" and "Bichota," inspiring millions with her music and becoming a role model for aspiring female artists.
Nicky Jam
Nicky Jam is a celebrated reggaeton artist whose career has spanned over two decades. Known for his smooth vocals and heartfelt lyrics, he has experienced a remarkable resurgence in the music industry with hits like "El Perdón" and "X." His music often reflects his personal struggles and triumphs, resonating with fans worldwide.
Ozuna
Ozuna is a Puerto Rican singer and songwriter recognized for his melodic voice and catchy hooks. He has taken the Latin music scene by storm with hits like "Taki Taki" and "Baila Baila Baila," often blending reggaeton with tropical influences, making him one of the most influential artists in Latin music today.
Maluma
Maluma is a Colombian singer and actor known for his romantic reggaeton and pop songs. With a smooth style and captivating stage presence, he has become a favorite among fans with hits like "Felices los 4" and "Hawái." Maluma's music often explores themes of love and relationships, appealing to a wide audience.
